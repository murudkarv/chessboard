*Description:
	-Two  player chess game which can be played between two humans. It supports only man vs man chess game.
	-No need of mouse to play this. You can use only keyboard to play the chess.
*requirements:
	-Basic requirements is you must have ubuntu OS installed on your computer.
	-Your system must have graphics.h library to run the graphics of the game.
*Save and Load the game:
	just follow the instructions on the screen.
*How to play:
	-Instructions on the screens will be sufficient. 
	-You have to just enter initial and final position in terms of given co-ordinates of your desired piece which you want move.
	-for castling just enter the positions,i.e.initial and final position of king your rook will move automatically if that castling is 		legal.
	-It is assumed that the human who are playing the game know the rules of chess.
	-reborn of queen and other pieces after pawn reaches to extreme position in itscolumn is not yet added.
	-rule of en-passant also not yet added.
how to install:
	-extract the folder in your home directory.
	-change the directory to my chess.
	-run these commands.
		make ./project
		./project 
	- enjoy the game.
